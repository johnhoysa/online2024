Hello, I'm a front-end developer with over 15 years experience working on responsive websites. I specialize in JavaScript, CSS, components, style guides, and content management systems.

Key Skills

My skills contribute to a well-rounded team member capable of tackling a variety of challenges.

Development

I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript daily to make responsive websites. Recently, I've been focused on learning Adobe Experience Manager and TypeScript. Additionally, I have started learning React because of its strong commitment to backwards compatibility, which ensures long-term reliability for companies.

Content Management

Whether it's a personal blog or an enterprise-level site, I can design and enhance content management systems (CMS) tailored to your specific needs. As a front-end developer, I excel in using template languages to present your content seamlessly or integrating content through APIs.

UX

I enjoy collaborating with design teams to bring their visions to life, ensuring pixel-perfect execution upon launch. I am equally adept at working independently, leveraging existing style guides to design and develop responsive components when a design team is not available.

Projects

Below are some past projects I have worked on.

Expedia

HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Adobe Experience Manager

As a Front-End Developer at Expedia Group, I maintain and enhance the Partner and Escapia marketing sites using Adobe Experience Manager (AEM). My role involves developing and refining AEM components, with a current focus on building a reusable set of components for various AEM-powered sites.

British American Tobacco

HTML, CSS, JS, Adobe Experience Manager, Magento

I developed accessible components, forms using JavaScript, CSS, and HTML for Adobe Experience Manager (AEM). Additionally, I built style guides and also integrated Magento product and user data into AEM components utilizing GraphQL and JavaScript.

Nescafé Dolce Gusto

HTML, CSS, JS, Magento

I leveraged Magento to develop and enhance websites for Nescafé Dolce Gusto's international markets. My responsibilities included resolving translation issues, layout issues, and installing and styling plugins to ensure a great user experience.

MacSales

HTML, CSS, JS, Craft CMS

I re-developed and integrated the ecommerce site of MacSales.com into Craft CMS, ensuring design and messaging consistency. I created single-page web applications for the Used Macs configurator and the My Upgrades section within the ecommerce platform, utilizing in-house APIs. Using the Neo Plugin I created a custom page builder allowing marketers to quickly and easily create new pages.

ESC 13

HTML, CSS, JS, ExpressionEngine CMS

At ESC 13 I built and maintained the organizations primary site and directed the web development project life cycle. One of my favorite projects was developing a video commenting web app for the video department. This tool significantly reduced the time required for video editing by streamlining feedback and collaboration. Another standout project was building an online conference platform. This platform featured registration, profile and networking capabilities, live video streaming, and user commenting, creating an engaging virtual experience.

The Parent Companion

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ExpressionEngine CMS

During my time at ESC 13, I had the privilege of developing the Parent Companion site. It was created for parents to help register their children with diagnosed or suspected disabilities to Texas schools. What stands out most about this project is the incredible passion and commitment from the volunteers. Their personal stories resonated with our team, offering the insights we needed to create a successful and user-friendly site to help parents along their journey.
